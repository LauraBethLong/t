My Idea of a Good Day

I love organizing a bunch of information into something beautiful, usable, understandable, and persuasive.

Wrangle a big spreadsheet to "keep the trains running on time."

Fill a screen with code and design a website, or something for print.

Chat with voters on social media, the phone, or at their front door. Learn what matters to them, and hopefully, help them learn why their support for my favorite candidates and causes matters.

Strategize how to better collect and use all this data and the internet to connect with voters and win.

Dig deep into researching a candidate or some policy issue. Write something about what I learned.

Before calling it a day, catch up with friends, cook dinner, and enjoy some simple pleasures: a good book, the company of a sweet cat curled up beside me, and a bit of figure skating.

Go to sleep to do it all again the next day for a cause I believe in.

I'm currently considering new projects for 2015 and 2016. You can hire me for a bit of freelance help here. If you'd like more help, say full or part time, email me. One last thing: Yes, a lot of people I work with are in politics, but I happily work with businesses and whatnot, too.
